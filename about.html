WHO WE ARE

  • IELTS Prepration
  • PTE Preparation
  • OET Preparation
  • English Language Proficiency

GSL Pakistan is an organization that offers preparation courses and testing services for the International English Language Testing System (IELTS). The IELTS is a widely recognized test that assesses the language proficiency of individuals who wish to study or work in English-speaking countries. The center is affiliated with the British Council, a UK-based organization that promotes cultural relations and educational opportunities worldwide. The British Council is also one of the owners of the IELTS test.
